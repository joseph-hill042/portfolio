Who Am I?

Declan a few weeks after birth

As I said before, I am foremost a husband and a father. The most difficult thing I ever faced in life was almost losing my son, Declan. He stopped breathing after birth and spent months in the NICU on a respirator.

Doctor's did not believe he would make it and that if he did that he would be in a vegetative state. I remember the day that my son's eyes finally opened and he looked at me. That is the moment that I began to believe in the impossible.

I watched my son defy all the odds that doctor's placed against him. He kept progressing and achieving milestones that we were told would be impossible for him.

Watching him inspired me to pursue achieving my dream of becoming a software developer. It had long been a hobby of mine and something I wanted to do, but told myself it was too late.

My son inspires me everyday to push myself to be the best I can be at everything I do. To not complain and be thankful for second chances. He is the strength behind every obstacle I overcome.

Declan July 2017
